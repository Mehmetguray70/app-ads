English

Last updated: 29 March 2026

+ This Privacy Policy explains what information may be processed when using the PriceCompare + mobile application and how that information may be used. +

+ +

1. Information We May Process

+
    +
  • Camera access and label photos captured by the user
    • +
  • Product names and prices detected through OCR
    • +
  • User preferences such as store currency, home currency, search country and app language
    • +
  • Exchange rate data used for currency conversion
    • +
  • Basic technical information required for ads and purchase functionality, when enabled
    • +
+ +

2. Why This Information Is Used

+
    +
  • To read product labels and compare prices
    • +
  • To restore user preferences when the app is reopened
    • +
  • To convert prices between selected currencies
    • +
  • To compare products against an official price list when available
    • +
  • To redirect users to Google Shopping when no official match is found
    • +
+ +

3. Photos and OCR Data

+

+ Label photos may be processed to extract product names and prices. Depending on the app + version, this processing may happen locally on the device and may not be permanently stored. +

+ +

4. Local Storage

+
    +
  • Selected currencies
    • +
  • Selected search country
    • +
  • Selected app language
    • +
  • Last retrieved exchange rate data
    • +
  • Cached official price list data
    • +
+ +

5. Third-Party Services

+
    +
  • Open exchange rate services
    • +
  • + Official price list source: + asotgames.com.tr/data/prices.json +
    • +
  • Google Shopping search
    • +
  • Google AdMob for advertising, if enabled in the app
    • +
  • Google Play Billing for premium purchases or subscriptions, if enabled in the app
    • +
+ +

6. Advertising and Purchases

+

+ The app may display advertising through Google AdMob and may offer premium purchases through + Google Play Billing. Google may process certain device and app identifiers as required for ad + delivery, fraud prevention, purchase validation, and billing operations. Please review Google's + own privacy terms for more information. +

+ +

7. Children’s Privacy

+

+ This app is not specifically directed to children. Children under 13 should use the app only + with parental or legal guardian supervision where required. +

+ +

8. Security

+

+ Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards are intended to protect information. + However, no internet transmission or electronic storage method can be guaranteed to be fully secure. +

+ +

9. Changes

+

+ This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always be published + on this page. +
