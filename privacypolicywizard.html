Bu Gizlilik Politikası, Rune Arena Wizard Survivor mobil oyununda hangi verilerin hangi amaçlarla işlendiğini açıklar. Oyun, temel oynanış için cihaz üzerinde kayıt tutar; Google girişi, bulut yedekleme, dereceli PVP, leaderboard, can senkronizasyonu ve ödüllü reklam gibi çevrimiçi özellikler kullanıldığında bazı veriler çevrimiçi servislerle işlenebilir.

Özetle: Oyunun temel ilerlemesi cihazda tutulur. Google hesabıyla giriş yapıldığında ilerleme, oyunun düşük maliyetli bulut yedekleme modeliyle Firestore'a yedeklenebilir. Normal oynanış sırasında her küçük değişiklik anında buluta yazılmaz; oyuncu uygulamayı silmeden önceki son oturumu tekrar açmadan kaldırırsa, o son oturumun cloud yedeği eksik kalabilir.

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what data may be processed in Rune Arena Wizard Survivor and for what purposes. The game stores core gameplay data on the device. When the player uses online features such as Google Sign-In, cloud backup, ranked PVP, leaderboard, lives synchronization, or rewarded ads, some data may be processed by online services.

In short: core progress is stored locally on the device. After Google Sign-In, progress may be backed up to Firestore using a low-cost cloud save model. The game does not upload every small progress change during normal play. If the player deletes the app before opening it again after a play session, the latest session may not be included in the cloud backup.

1. Controller and Contact

The developer of Rune Arena Wizard Survivor is the contact point for the data processing described in this policy. For privacy and data protection questions, please contact: mehmetguray.yucesan@gmail.com

2. Data We May Process

Depending on the features used, the following categories of data may be processed:

  • Locally stored game progress, unlocked levels, stars, best scores, gem/soul stone balance, upgrade levels, selected and unlocked weapons, selected and unlocked character cosmetics, language, audio, and preference settings.
  • When Google Sign-In is used: Firebase user ID, email address, Google display name, and the nickname chosen in the game. Profile photo URL is not stored in the game database.
  • For cloud backup in Firestore: level progress, scores, stars, gem/soul stone balance, upgrades, weapons, cosmetics, nickname, language preference, and update time.
  • For the lives system: current lives, maximum lives, last life change time, and update time.
  • For leaderboard: player ID, display name/nickname, total score, and highest reached level.
  • For ranked PVP: rank level, tier, division, match count, wins, losses, draws, streaks, best rank, recent results, and reward gem information.
  • For online PVP matchmaking and room management in Firebase Realtime Database: user ID, player name, loadout summary, rank information, room status, ready state, and technical timestamps.
  • For rewarded ads: advertising identifiers, device information, ad impression data, and ad interaction data that may be processed by Google Mobile Ads / AdMob.
  • Remote configuration data loaded from asotgames.com.tr for weekly missions and mandatory update checks. Standard technical access logs may be created by the hosting server for those requests.
  • Technical data needed for app version checks, device/operating system compatibility, connectivity, debugging, security, and service continuity.

The game does not request access to camera, microphone, contacts, precise location, health data, or personal files on the device.

3. Purposes of Processing

  • To operate the game, save progress, and restore progress across installations.
  • To manage Google Sign-In, nickname, and profile flows.
  • To provide leaderboard, ranked PVP, online PVP, matchmaking, and room management features.
  • To operate the lives system, rewards, gem/soul stone balance, weapon, cosmetic, and upgrade unlocks.
  • To show rewarded ads and grant ad rewards.
  • To provide weekly missions, remote content, and mandatory update checks.
  • To maintain security, prevent abuse, debug issues, ensure service continuity, and comply with legal obligations.

4. Legal Basis

Data that may qualify as personal data may be processed based on the performance of a service requested by the user, legitimate interests, explicit consent where required, and compliance with legal obligations.

5. Third-Party Services

Some game features rely on third-party services. These services may process data under their own privacy policies:

  • Google Sign-In and Firebase Authentication: Google account sign-in and user authentication.
  • Firebase Cloud Firestore: profile, cloud progress backup, lives, leaderboard, and ranked PVP data.
  • Firebase Realtime Database: online PVP matchmaking, lobby/room, signaling, and temporary match data.
  • Google Mobile Ads / AdMob: ad serving, rewarded ads, ad measurement, and abuse prevention.
  • Google Play and Apple App Store: app distribution, downloads, store operations, and platform security.
  • asotgames.com.tr: weekly mission configuration and app version/update information.

You can review the relevant service provider policies at Google Privacy Policy, Firebase Privacy and Security, and Google AdMob policy information.

6. Retention and Deletion

Data stored on the device may remain until the user deletes the app or clears app data. Cloud data linked to a Google account may be retained for as long as needed to provide the relevant features, maintain security, resolve issues, or comply with legal obligations. A user may contact us to request deletion of game data linked to their account.

7. Data Security

Firestore and Realtime Database access is limited by security rules. Users can access their own profile, progress, lives, and ranked data; for PVP and leaderboard features, limited data needed for the feature may be visible to other users. No internet-based system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

8. International Transfers

Services such as Firebase, Google Mobile Ads, Google Play, and Apple App Store may process data on servers outside the user's country. Such transfers are handled under the relevant provider's security and privacy safeguards and applicable law.

9. Children's Privacy

The game is a general entertainment game and does not intentionally request direct identity information from children. If a parent or legal guardian believes that a child's personal data has been processed, they may contact us.

10. Your Rights

Depending on applicable law, you may have the right to request information about personal data processed about you, request correction or deletion, object to certain processing activities, and exercise other rights provided by applicable data protection laws.

11. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The updated version becomes effective when published.
